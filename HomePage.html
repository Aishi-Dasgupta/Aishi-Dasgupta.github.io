I am a student at IIT Bombay, currently pursuing Masters in Biotechnology in my 2nd and final year. I have been fascinated by Genetic Engineering and Molecular Biology since early Bachelor Days and hence I aim to work in this field in the future. I have a vision to strive to incorporate my skills in attempting to solve intriguing and complex questions. I also intend to utilise Bioinformatics, Programming and Machine Learning to give a different direction to my approach.

I started my journey with an internship in a Stem Cell Lab where I worked on marking the splenic niche of Hematopoietic Stem Cekks with Lyve 1 in conjunction with Periostin. This was in connection to an ongoing project that aimed to decipher the complexities behind Integrin-Periostin Signalling. Currently, I am doing my Masters Thesis Project where I aim to apply Bioinfomatics, Biostatistics and Genetic Engineering tools to create a database of Base Excision Repair Enzymes in large DNA viruses and make a phylogenetic analysis with sequence comparison and regression analysis.
